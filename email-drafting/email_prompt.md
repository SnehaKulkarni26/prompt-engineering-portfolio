# Email Drafting Prompt

## Goal
Write a simple and professional email for an internship request.

## Prompt (Version 1)
"Write a short and polite email asking for a Prompt Engineering internship."

## Output (Version 1)
Subject: Internship Request – Prompt Engineering

Dear Sir/Madam,

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to express my interest in applying for a Prompt Engineering internship with your organization. I am eager to learn and develop my skills in working with AI tools and prompt design.

This internship would be a great opportunity for me to gain practical experience and improve my understanding of prompt engineering concepts. I am highly motivated, quick to learn, and willing to put in my best efforts.

I would be grateful if you could consider my request. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Kind regards,
Sneha


## Observation (Version 1)
- Polite wording created a professional tone.
- Short length kept the email clear.

---

## Prompt (Version 2 – Small Change)
"Write a short and professional email asking for a Prompt Engineering internship. Use a formal tone."

## Output (Version 2)
Subject: Application for Prompt Engineering Internship

Dear Sir/Madam,

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to formally express my interest in applying for a Prompt Engineering internship with your organization. I am keen to gain practical exposure to prompt design and AI-based systems while enhancing my technical and analytical skills.

I believe this internship would provide valuable learning opportunities and allow me to contribute meaningfully while developing a strong foundation in prompt engineering. I am dedicated, detail-oriented, and eager to learn.

I would appreciate your consideration of my application. Thank you for your time and attention.

Yours sincerely,
Sneha


## Observation (Version 2)
- Formal tone made the email more professional.
- Clear role mention improved relevance.

---

## What I Learned
- Tone instructions strongly affect email style.
- Clear prompts improve professionalism.
